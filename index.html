How It Works

Simple to set up. Easier to use.

Neo connects to your existing business phone number. When a customer calls or texts, Neo handles it — professionally and promptly — so you can focus on the job.

Step 1

Connect your number

We set up Neo on your existing business phone number. No new hardware, no complicated software.

Step 2

Neo answers every call

When a customer calls or texts, Neo greets them professionally, answers common questions, and takes a message if needed.

Step 3

You get notified

You receive a summary of every interaction — who called, what they needed, and any follow-up required.

Step 4

You call back when ready

Handle callbacks on your schedule. Your customers are taken care of, and no lead falls through the cracks.

Step 5

Keep your existing number

Already have a business number your customers know? Keep it. Simply set up call forwarding from your existing number to Neo — takes about 60 seconds from your phone.

Spam Protection

Live Spam‑Free Communication

We don't bill for spam. We stop it.

Most call services make you pay for junk — robocalls, ringless voicemail drops, silent hang‑ups. We built our service differently.

✅ Spam Isn't Usage. It's Noise.

Our platform actively filters spam calls and texts before they ever become billable. If a call is identified as spam, it's blocked or ended automatically — and you're never charged for it.

That means:

  • — No minutes wasted on robocalls
  • — No alerts for junk calls
  • — No surprise overages from spam traffic

  • ✓ Silent calls and instant hang‑ups filtered out
