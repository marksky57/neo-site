How It Works

Easy to set up. Even easier to use.

Neo connects to your current phone number. When a customer calls or texts, Neo handles it — professionally and quickly — so you can stay focused on your work.

Step 1

Connect your number

We set Neo up on your current phone number. No new equipment or complicated software.

Step 2

Neo answers every call

When a customer calls or texts, Neo greets them professionally, answers common questions, and takes a message if needed.

Step 3

You get notified

You get a summary of every interaction — who called, what they needed, and whether it requires follow-up.

Step 4

Call back when you can

Return calls on your schedule. Your customers are taken care of and no lead falls through the cracks.

Step 5

Keep your existing number

Already have a number your customers know? Keep it. Just set up call forwarding from your number to Neo — it takes about 60 seconds from your phone.

What "Spam Prevention" Actually Means

It's not a generic blocklist. It's active, behavioral protection:

  • ✓ Robocalls and automated messages blocked automatically
  • ✓ Silent calls and instant hangups filtered
  • ✓ Repeat spam numbers blocked across the network
  • ✓ Spam messages ignored — no response, no charge
  • ✓ Owner notifications only for legitimate calls
